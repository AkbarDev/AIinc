Scoring inputs

Trend score = Σ weighted signals

We prioritize coverage breadth, velocity, and trust. Signals are recalculated each time the ingestion job runs (usually every 15 minutes via GitHub Actions).

score = (source × 0.30) + (recency × 0.25) + (keywords × 0.20) + (authority × 0.15) + (engagement × 0.10)
  • Source: distinct publishers referencing the same topic
  • Recency: time decay with 6h half-life
  • Keywords: AI, semiconductor, streaming, etc.
  • Authority: feed credibility tier
  • Engagement: heuristic boost for AI/GPT, blockbuster IP, esports finales
