    Trend score = weighted editorial signals

    We prioritize coverage breadth, velocity, and trust. Signals are recalculated each time the ingestion job runs so the homepage stays fresh without becoming visually noisy.

    score = (source × 0.30) + (recency × 0.25) + (keywords × 0.20) + (authority × 0.15) + (engagement × 0.10)
    • Source: distinct publishers referencing the same topic
    • Recency: time decay with 6h half-life
    • Keywords: AI, infrastructure, enterprise, commerce
    • Authority: feed credibility tier
    • Engagement: boost for models, platforms, and enterprise adoption stories
